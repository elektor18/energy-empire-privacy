Privacy Policy for Energy Empire

Last updated: December 2025

Thank you for playing Energy Empire. This privacy policy explains how the game handles data on your device.

1. Overview

Energy Empire is a mobile strategy game that runs primarily on your device. The game does not collect personal information or transmit gameplay data to external servers operated by the developer.

2. Data We Collect

Energy Empire itself does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

However, the game uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK, which may collect the following data for ad delivery and personalization:

  • Device identifiers (for ad frequency capping and attribution)
  • Approximate location (for ad relevance)

These data are collected and processed by Google according to their own privacy policies. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The game stores only the following information locally on your device (via Android's app storage):

  • Game progress (tile states, upgrades, production)
  • Player statistics
  • Settings
  • Daily rewards and streak timestamps
  • Ad watches and rewarded-ad counters
  • Pending operations (build, claim, upgrade)

This data never leaves your device and is accessible only to the game app (sandboxed by the OS).

4. Third‑Party Services

Google Mobile Ads SDK is used to display banner, interstitial and rewarded ads. Google may collect certain device information according to their privacy practices. You can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.

We do not use analytics services, crash reporting, or other third‑party trackers by default. If additional services are integrated in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Children’s Privacy

Energy Empire is suitable for all ages (recommended PEGI/ESRB: Everyone). The game does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Security

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data stored on our servers. Local save data is stored in the Android app sandbox. Communication with ad servers is encrypted by the ad provider.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy on the official project page and update the Last updated date at the top of this page.
